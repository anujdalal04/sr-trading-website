Complete Fire Protection

A firefighting pump system is integral to building safety, ensuring that water is delivered at the required pressure to sprinklers and hydrants during an emergency. These systems are designed to operate reliably under extreme conditions.

  • Main Pump: Electric driven pump that provides the primary water flow.
  • Jockey Pump: Maintains system pressure to prevent unnecessary cycling of the main pump.
  • Standby Pump: Diesel-driven backup for power failure situations.
